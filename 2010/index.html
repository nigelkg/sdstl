The clubs in the following table are affiliated to the South Devon Summer Tennis League, which plays its matches between April and September. There are four competitions, a mixed league competition which is supported by three divisions, a Ladies and a Mens league divisions. These League divisions are sponsored by Truprint.

Also there is a mixed Knockout Tournament which is sponsored by Geoff Date.

The club locations are shown on Google Map links under the location links.

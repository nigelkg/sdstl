The AGM is on 15th April at 7:30pm at the Meadowbrook Community Centre. The listings are preliminary for checking.

The clubs in the following table are affiliated to the South Devon Summer Tennis League, which plays its matches between April and September. There are four competitions, a mixed league competition which is supported by three divisions, a Ladies and a Mens league divisions. These League divisions are sponsored by WBW Solicitors.

Also there is a mixed Knockout Tournament which is sponsored by Geoff Date.

The club locations are shown on Google Map links under the location links.

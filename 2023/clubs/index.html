The clubs in the following table are affiliated to the South Devon Summer Tennis League, which plays its matches between April and September. There are four competitions, a mixed league competition which is supported by three divisions, a Ladies and a Mens league divisions.

Also there is a mixed Knockout Tournament which is sponsored by Geoff Date.

The club locations are shown on Google Map links under the location links.

The AGM was held on 4th April 2023 at Newton Abbot Tennis Club.
